SECTION 11: ADMISSIONS PROCESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1 — Complete the Application Form:
  - Submit academic qualifications
  - Provide JEE score or SAT score
  - Include extracurricular activities

Step 2 — Submit the Video Essay:
  - 2-minute video essay
  - Topics: Interest in technology, past building experiences, motivation for DS & AI programme
  - Time to complete: ~7 days after invitation

Step 3 — Take the Aptitude Test:
  - Option A: Upload JEE Advanced admit card or SAT scores
  - Option B: Take the Masters' Union Scholarship Aptitude Test (MUSAT)
  - MUSAT window: 20 days to complete
