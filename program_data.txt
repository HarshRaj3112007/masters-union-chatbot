Can students submit their documents/ certificates later if they need
                                        time to get it from school?
Students can fill the form with certificates that are
                                    available. Later on, they can simply provide the remaining documents to the team as the
                                    application processes.
Is it mandatory for students to submit their class XI marksheet?
Yes, it is. However, if they don’t have the Class XI marksheet
                                    (PCM), they can upload an application explaining the reason for its unavailability at the
                                    time of filling out the application and attach the PDF in place of the marksheet.
If a student has lost their X result (or any document) which is
                                        mandatory to upload, how can students fill the form?
They can upload an application stating the reason for not
                                    having it under the 10th
                                    marksheet upload section. Ensure to send an official email at
admission@mastersunion.org
later, once the
                                    students obtain it.
Can students edit the video essay?
Yes, they can but it is not needed. We are
                                    judging students on their communication and confidence and not their video editing skills.
Is there a sample video essay?
Yes, here is a
sample video
.
When will students get their application results and get shortlisted
                                        for the further round?
Results can be expected to be announced
                                    anywhere between 15-30 days after the commencement of the deadline. Post receiving
                                    information on the shortlist, students will receive an online Scholarship (Aptitude) Test
                                    that they have to take online from home.
What if a student has submitted the video but didn't get the
                                        Scholarship (Aptitude) Test mail yet?
The email will be sent once the video is
                                    evaluated and students are selected. Results can be expected to be announced anywhere
                                    between 15-30 days after the submission of the video. Find more details here.
here
.
What are the passing marks?
We look into the profile holistically
                                    before selecting or rejecting a student. However, please note that scoring 35 marks (out of
                                    a total of 45) is the eligibility criteria for scholarship opportunities.
Can students submit their JEE/SAT/CUET Score instead of taking the
                                        Masters’ Union Scholarship (Aptitude) Test?
Yes, students also have the option to
                                    provide their JEE/IPMAT/SAT/CUET scores. The Scholarship (Aptitude) Test becomes optional
                                    for those who have provided their scores and they will be shortlisted on the basis of the
                                    score. Please note that the scholarship test is not just for students looking for
                                    scholarships but is also an essential step of our admission process which enables us to
                                    assess the analytical capabilities of the student.
How much time do students get to prepare for the Scholarship
                                        (Aptitude) Test?
Students will get a 7-day period to finish the Scholarship (Aptitude) Test. Find more
                                        details
here
.
Can students use calculators?
Yes, students are allowed to use
                                    calculators. We suggest they keep a pen and paper handy with them during the test and a
                                    glass of water.
Is there any negative marking?
There is no negative marking.
What is considered a good JEE/IPMAT/CUET percentile?
Percentile greater than or equal to 90 is
                                    considered good.
What is a good SAT score?
Students who choose to submit their SAT
                                    scores, a score greater than or equal to 1500 is considered good. Please note the test score
                                    is one aspect of the application. Student profiles are evaluated holistically.
Can students get an extension to appear for the Scholarship
                                        (Aptitude) test?
They can write an email to the
ugadmissions@mastersunion.org , attaching the specific documents
                                    explaining why an extension is needed. Extension can be granted in genuine cases, as per
                                    consideration via email.
When will the results for the Scholarship (Aptitude) Test be
                                        announced?
Results can be expected to be announced
                                    anywhere between 15-30 days after the test deadline.
Is the business simulation/ personal interview conducted online or
                                        offline?
The in-person business simulation and
                                    interview will be conducted on the same day for a student. These will be scheduled across
                                    major cities (Delhi, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Indore, Hyderabad, Chandigarh) in India
                                    and over Zoom in exceptional cases.
What is GD or business simulation ? How to prepare for it?
The in-person business simulation will
                                    require students to role-play as a CXO of a company and solve its business challenges in a
                                    team of 5-6 applicants. We will send them a case study to prepare for the business
                                    simulation round 3-4 days prior for preparation. In this round, they will be judged on their
                                    creativity, communication, and teamwork skills. They will be participating in a group
                                    discussion for 45 minutes followed by a presentation (without any slides).
What happens in the personal interview, what kind of questions will be
                                        asked?
The interview will be a one-on-one
                                    conversation with our faculty or senior leadership. The questions will be around their
                                    application and motivations for applying to Masters' Union. The goal is to know more about
                                    students and what they want to achieve at Masters' Union.
Is there any detailed information about GD/PI ?
The overall process will take around 3 hours.
The business simulation will last 45 minutes followed by a 15 minute group
                                                    presentation. Students will be working in teams of 4-6 students on a
                                                    simulated
                                                    business scenario, where students will be expected to make decisions,
                                                    analyse
                                                    data and present their findings.
The interview will be conducted by a senior leader or a faculty member at
                                                    Masters’ Union and will be approximately 30 minutes long. They will be asked
                                                    questions about their application including their achievements, essays and
                                                    the
                                                    video essay submitted.
Dress code for the event is smart casuals.
When will the results be declared?
The results are announced 30-45 days after
                                    the date of the interview. You can find more details
here
.
Is there a firm deadline for payment of the admission fee?
We provide students ample time till July
                                    of the academic year so that they can make their decision based on all the admission offers
                                    that they have. In case they still want an extension, the same can be granted based on an
                                    email request.
What is the mode of payment for the fees?
Mode of payment can be online, net
                                    banking, bank transfer or in some rare cases they can even send a cheque. Except for cash,
                                    we accept all payment methods.
What is the fee payment schedule?
Students can pay the fees semester wise.
                                    Starting every semester, they will be notified about the fees due by
ugadmissions@mastersunion.org
Does Masters’ Union offer scholarships?
At Masters’ Union we believe that financial constraints should
                                    not pose any hindrance to students’ academic aspirations. We have merit and need-based
                                    scholarships for all deserving candidates. Students scoring above 35 out of 45 marks in the
                                    Masters’ Union Scholarship Aptitude test get a scholarship which can range from 25% - 80% of
                                    the tuition fee depending on the student’s performance in the Scholarship (Aptitude)Test and
                                    the overall profile of the applicant. For candidates keen on scholarship, we encourage
                                    students to submit their application early to allow for our admission counsellors to
                                    thoroughly review their scholarship case before presenting it to the admissions committee at
                                    the time of evaluation.
Is the scholarship for all four years or just for the first year?
Scholarship is awarded for all four years. If a student
                                    receives a 20% scholarship, they will receive a 20% scholarship every semester on their
                                    respective fees. However, in cases of misconduct or rule violations, disciplinary action may
                                    be taken.
Are there any financing options?
Yes, there are financial institutions that can provide loans
                                    for up to 95% of the programme fees to the admitted students. Read
here
for further
                                    details.
